Net Skip is a thoughtfully designed web browser that brings clarity and speed to everyday browsing.

Built on the system's native web engine, Net Skip renders pages quickly and scrolls smoothly, with an interface that quietly steps aside as you read. The result is a browser that feels light, responsive, and out of the way — letting you focus on the page rather than the chrome around it.


FEATURES

— Fast page loads and fluid scrolling, powered by the native web engine
— A clean, uncluttered interface that recedes while you read
— Swipe between tabs with a single gesture
— Visual tab overview with live previews of every open page
— Automatic session restore — your tabs are right where you left them
— Built-in ad and tracker blocking, on by default
— A choice of search engines, with suggestions as you type
— Find-on-page, adjustable text zoom, and a request-desktop-site option
— Favorites and full browsing history, with simple controls to clear what you wish
— Light, dark, and automatic appearance modes
— Optional haptic feedback for a more tactile experience
— JavaScript and custom user-agent settings for advanced users


PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Net Skip is built around a simple idea: your browsing belongs to you.

— No advertising of any kind
— No tracking, analytics, or telemetry
— No in-app purchases or subscriptions
— No accounts, no sign-in, no profile

What happens in Net Skip stays on your device.


OPEN SOURCE

Net Skip is fully open source. The complete source code is published openly so anyone can read it, audit it, or contribute. You can find the project at https://github.com/Net-Skip.
